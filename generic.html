Academics

I did my schooling from St.Mary's High School, Kalyan from 2005-2014 and passed out 10th Std with 94.00%. Pursued Science from KET's V.G Vaze College of Arts, Science and Commerce, Mulund and passed out in 2016 with 89.67%. Owing to my increased fascination with Mobile Apps and a certain MKBHD, I took up Computer Engineering from Terna Engineering College and passed out with 9.15 CGPA in 2020.

With that in mind, I appeared for CAT and other MBA entrance exams scoring 98.70 ( 99.99 in VARC ) in CAT, 99.54 in XAT, 98.95 in IIFT followed by a 99.45 in SNAP. Super stoked and happy to share that I will be joining XLRI Jamshedpur for their MBA programme for the academic year 2023-25. Looking forward to start again from zero and create experiences to cherish for a lifetime.
